About Us

Recycling. Simplified.

At Recycling Otago, we aim to have a solution for all of your recycling problems.

In Otago, the main bodies responsible for recycling collection and management are the local councils. However, there are many small recycling centers that exist which operate individually from the councils.

We offer a range of information for all kinds of recycling in the Otago region.
